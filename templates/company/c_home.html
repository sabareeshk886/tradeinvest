Portfolio Management and Trading

Portfolio management and trading involve strategically managing investment portfolios to optimize returns and manage risks. This includes asset selection, diversification, performance monitoring, and executing trades. Traders actively buy and sell securities to take advantage of market opportunities and align with investment objectives. The goal is to achieve financial objectives by effectively managing and trading within the portfolio.
